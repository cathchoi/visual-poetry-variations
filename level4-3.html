Hearts Like Wildflowers

I hope you are blessed
With a Heart like a Wildflower
Strong enough to rise again

After being
Trampled upon
Tough enough

To Weather the Worst of the Summer Storms
And able to
Grow

&
Flourish
Even in the

Most
Broken
Places.

Nikita Gill
